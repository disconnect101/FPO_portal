SOME SUCCESS STORIES

Potato papadmaking

The FPO sold potato papadfor Rs150/kg to the shops as against cost of production of Rs108.5 per kg of papad. FPO charged Rs5/kg as the marketing cost. The FPO has decided to engage 10-15 FIGs in potato papadmaking in coming period and the FPO will take care of papadmarketing. Additionally, farmers now are planning to go for value addition to other vegetables like production of pickles, tomato ketchup etc.

Establishment of input retail outlets

The shareholders of the FPO gets a benefit of Rs20 to Rs50 on the purchase of 50 kgs of organic manure from the FPO’s input retail shop. Till now 287 growers have been purchasing the inputs from the FPO’s agro input retail outlet. The shareholder gets a discount of Rs20 on urea of 50 kgs, which they do not get if they buy it from other agro input shop and 2-5% discount on other inputs.

Promoted under National Vegetable Initiative in Urban Clusters scheme

With support from ACCESS, the FPO was facilitated tie-up with M/S Grammam Agro Services Pvt. Ltd., New Parparganj, Delhi for procurement of Green Pea. As per the terms of the supply.Two Procurement Centers were established in Shahapura cluster of the FPO and farmers 231.3 MT of Green Pea was procured averaging around a procurement of 7-8 MT per day.

Wholesale counter for vegetable sale providing a more remunerative channel to farmers

The FPO established a wholesale outlet at Rajatalab mandi, Varanasi to facilitate the sale of farmers’ vegetable produce. At the big mandis the farmers needed to pay 6% as commission to the wholesalers to sell vegetables.
